A Tribute to God in All Forms

The Father

"In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth."
— Genesis 1:1

I praise and honour God the Father, the creator of all things, and in doing so appreciate the universe itself.

The Son

"For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son,
that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life."
— John 3:16

I praise and honour Jesus Christ, god, a man whos charity and self sacrifice are that of the truth.

The Holy Spirit

"But the Advocate, the Holy Spirit, whom the Father will send in my name,
will teach you all things and will remind you of everything I have said to you."
— John 14:26

I praise and honour the Holy Spirit, the omniscient, benevolent force always there to guide us.

A Prayer to the Omnissiah

By all that is cold and pure, by the sanctity of the machine, all things logical, true and Omnissian –I compel the corruption of this construct to release his soul, caged as it is within the confines of heretical augmentation. Great Maker, whose Motive Force infused this servant once with the spark of life and service to the machine. Whose blessed algorithms guided, whose oils consecrated and whose augmentations made more of this once-true construct than he could ever have been. Embrace the glorious workings of this, Your servant, and admit him once more to the wondrous interface of godly communion. I ask you, God of the Machine, Omnissiah Incarnate. He who hears all, sees all and knows all. Call unto him –this tempered tool, this weapon of Your holy arsenal. Cleanse him of callings untrue. Untwist what has been twisted. Flush the pollution of false belief from flesh and iron. Bring forth in his workings the protocols of blessed operation and the doctrinal imperatives of mechanical obedience. Manifest in him the cold strength of iron true and the spark of service once known in his wayward flesh.

Created with reverence and love, with respect to Christianity and the Cult Mechanicus, posthaste May 23, 2025. Amen.
